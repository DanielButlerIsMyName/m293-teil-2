About gibb

gibb (Gewerblich-Industrielle Berufsbildung Bern) is one of the largest vocational schools in Switzerland, offering high-quality education and training to students in various trades and industries.

Our Campus

Located in the heart of Bern, our modern campus provides a collaborative learning environment with state-of-the-art facilities and resources tailored for practical education.

Innovative Learning

Our programs blend theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience, ensuring our students are well-prepared for successful careers in their chosen fields.

ICT Specialist

Our ICT Specialist program equips students with a comprehensive understanding of Information and Communication Technology. Students will develop skills in network administration, software development, and system management.

  • Duration: 3 years
  • Hands-on experience with the latest technologies
  • Opportunity for internships in leading IT companies
Automotive Technician

The Automotive Technician program provides students with hands-on training in vehicle maintenance, diagnostics, and repair. You'll learn about engine systems, transmissions, brakes, and more.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Certification upon completion
  • Practical workshops in state-of-the-art garages
Commercial Clerk

The Commercial Clerk program prepares students for a career in business administration, providing expertise in office management, accounting, and business communications.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Focus on practical business skills
  • Career opportunities in various industries
